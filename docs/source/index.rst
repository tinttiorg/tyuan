The World of Dr. Ting Yuan
=================================
.. image:: image/uni_t_logo.jpg
    :width: 100px
    :align: left
    :alt: T Logo




This is the homepage of Dr. Ting Yuan, an autonomous system scientist specializing in Sensor Fusion, Object Tracking, Aero Tech, and Automotive Radar Perception Systems. 


`Google Scholar <http://scholar.google.com/citations?user=hFp6u2UAAAAJ>`_, Sunnyvale CA.

---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

The World carries both individual and collective purposes:

-  Individually, to share my domain knowledge learned from the fusion Maestro Dr. `Yaakov Bar Shalom`_, my *BABY* Dr. `Peter K Willett`_, and fellow researchers of `ISIF`_/`Automatica`_ societies, such as Dr. `Anders Lindquist`_, Dr. `Peter Luh`_ (`RIP`_!) and Dr. `Jianxun Li`_, and industrial experience from many former colleages and friends at the Mercedes-Benz RD of North America and Germany, such as Dr. `Axel Gern`_, Dr. `Luca Del Grossi`_, Dr. `Juergen Dickmann`_, and Dr. `Martin Fritzsche`_.   
-  Collectively, to talk and discuss in the autonomous society on specific topics with distinguished researchers, such as Dr. `Perry Wang`_ and Dr. `Alexandre Alahi`_, and my interns and friends, such as Dr. `Bharanidhar Duraisamy`_, Dr. `Simon Romanski`_, and Dr. `Krishanth Krishnan`_.
